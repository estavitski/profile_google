User Registration

Please ensure that all team members participating in the experiment have valid BNL Guest registrations.
If registration has not been completed or has expired, submit new or renewal requests as soon as possible. The approval process can take between two weeks and up to six months.

In addition, a valid BNL computer account is now required. Instructions can be found here. Failure to obtain network credentials and install Duo Federal on your mobile device may prevent participation or data access.

Safety Approval Form (SAF)

Submit the SAF no later than two weeks before your scheduled start date. The form must:

  • Accurately describe the experiment and all materials used (samples, chemicals, solvents, reference materials, and equipment)
  • Include details about any equipment provided by the user group
  • Clearly outline all standard operating procedures and necessary safety precautions

Incomplete or late SAF submissions may result in the loss of beamtime.

Please note that the Guest, User & Visitor Center is moving to the Science and User Support Center located near the Laboratory entrance.

Additional Review Requirements

If your research is funded by or affiliated with any of the following agencies, please contact beamline staff immediately:

  • NASA
  • DOE/NNSA
  • NSA
  • DHS
  • DoD agencies (e.g., DARPA, DTRA, ONR, AFRL)
  • Projects involving national security, defense, space, or radiological/nuclear materials

An additional review may be required.

Training

Complete all required online user training at least one week prior to your experiment.

For further details, please consult the NSLS-II User Guide or contact ISS beamline staff directly.
